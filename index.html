About SelfOS

What is SelfOS?

SelfOS is a comprehensive personal productivity and discipline system designed to help you track, manage, and achieve your goals. Built entirely in your browser with zero backend servers, it keeps all your data private and secure.

Key Features

  • Daily Task Management - Add tasks with priorities and track completion in real-time.
  • Goal Tracking - Set long-term goals with deadlines and monitor progress visually.
  • Course Progress - Track your learning journey lesson by lesson.
  • Daily Reflection - Journal your learnings and areas for improvement with mood tracking.
  • Analytics Dashboard - View comprehensive charts and statistics of your productivity.
  • Gamification - Earn points, unlock badges, and maintain streaks for motivation.
  • Library Tracker - Monitor your reading progress across multiple books.
  • NoFap Challenge - Track daily discipline with a visual calendar system.
  • Planning & Reflection - Plan tomorrow's goals and reflect on daily achievements.
